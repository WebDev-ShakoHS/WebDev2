Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Integrate the liquid ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, integrate the dry ingredients.
  3. Integrate both mixtures until you get a homogeneous mixture. If it is a little thick, nothing happens.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat, add a little butter to grease, pour a little of the mixture into the pan to form the pancakes. I like to use a 1/4 measuring cup so they are all about the same size.
  5. Wait for it to heat and brown a little, turn and brown the other side.
  6. Serve with butter, honey, and your favorite fruits!

Molletes

Ingredients

  • Bolillo rolls, sliced in half lengthwise
  • 3 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • 1 can refried beans, heated
  • 1 package Cheddar
  • Mexican salsa or pico de gallo, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F . Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Remove some of the bread from the middle of bread halves to accommodate more toppings. Spread about 1 teaspoon butter on each half.
  3. Spread 2 to 3 tablespoons warmed refried beans on bread. Sprinkle about 1/4 cup Cheddar shredded cheese on the beans. Place bread halves on prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the bread is crispy, 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Top molletes with Mexican salsa or pico de gallo.

Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 handful of sauteed mushrooms
  • 1 salt, pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. In a pan over medium heat melt the butter and wait for it to start
  2. Meanwhile, place the eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl, beat for 20 seconds until well mixed.
  3. Pour into the hot buttered pan, let cook until you start to see that the edge is cooked, start moving the pan to make sure that the omelette is loose and has already formed a crust.
  4. Add the filling and let the cheese begin to melt. If you are going to fold it in half, you can put stuffing over the whole mixture.
  5. Using a spatula, fold the omelette and move to a hopefully hot plate.

Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped. flour tortillas
  • 1/2 pound pork sausage
  • Pico De Gallo Salsa
  • eggs

Instructions

  1. In a medium skillet cook pork, stirring frequently, until fully cooked.
  2. Add the bell pepper and onion to the meat. Cook for 1 minute and drain the meat.
  3. Beat the eggs and pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Cook the mixture, with the meat.
  4. Put the mixture in the tortilla Roll the tortillas so that the filling is inside. Top each burrito with your favorite Pico de Gallo salad and serve.
