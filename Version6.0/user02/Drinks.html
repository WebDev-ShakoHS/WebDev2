Easy to prepare and delicious drinks

Smothie

Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 banana
  • 1 c. frozen strawberries
  • 1 c. frozen blackberries, plus more for garnish (optional)
  • 1 c. frozen raspberries
  • 1 1/4 c. almond milk
  • 1/2 c. Greek yogurt
    		•
    • In a blender, combine all ingredients and blend until smooth.
    • Divide between 2 cups and top with blackberries, if desired.

    Chamoyada

  • 1 cup diced fresh mango, frozen
  • 1 can pineapple juice
  • ½ lime
  • 2 teaspoons chili-lime seasoning (such as Tajin)
  • 2 tablespoons chamoy (Mexican chili fruit condiment)
    		•
    • Combine frozen mangoes and pineapple juice in a blender; blend until smooth.
    • Rub a lime wedge around the edge of a glass and dip it in 1 teaspoon chili lime seasoning.
    • Squeeze lime into glass
    • Add 1 tablespoon chamoy and 1 teaspoon chili lime seasoning.
    • Pour in half of the mango and pineapple mixture
    • Add the remaining chamoy sauce and top with the remaining mango and pineapple mixture.

    Smoothie with ice cream

  • 1 liter of cold milk
  • 250 grams of ice cream
  • 4 tablespoons of Sugar .
    		•
    • Pour the cold milk into a blender along with the sugar and mix well.
    • Then add the ice cream of your preferred flavor and continue beating until you get a thick and foamy mixture.
    • Serve and eat quickly so that it does not lose its texture.

    Hot mocha

  • 300 mL whole milk
  • 100 g dark chocolate .
  • 20g instant coffee
  • 100mL of whipping cream
  • 2 red cherries in syrup
  • 30 g of dark chocolate
    		•
    • Heat the milk with the dark chocolate until it dissolves completely
    • Add the instant coffee and beat the mixture.
    • Melt the dark chocolate and cover the cherries with it.
    • Serve the mocha in the glass cup or glass, and decorate with the whipped cream and a chocolate cherry.
