Cheese rolls, and bacon

You only need cheese and bacon.

To prepare them we have to put the cheese on a toothpick and then we are going to roll it with the bacon, then we simply bake for 5 minutes and that's it.


Fried banana balls

You need banana and chesse, and if you want you can use nutella or jams.

To prepare it you must undo the banana until it is pure, then put a little bit in your hand and put a little piece of cheese on it then make the ball, make more balls, and put them in the oil for a few minutes, take them out and that's it! !.

Cheese fingers

We need mozzarella cheese in the form of a stick or finger, flour, egg and breadcrumbs

First we have the cheese in the flour until everything is covered, then we put it in the egg, then only in the ground bread and once it is ready we put it in the hot oil for a few minutes and that's it.

Sausage flags with flour tortilla

We will use sausages and flour tortillas, and if you want ketchup and cheese

It is very simple we just roll the sausage in the flour tortilla, and put it in the hot oil until we see that they are already stuck (not for long so that it does not burn)

Bacon covered sausages

We just roll the sausages with the bacon, and put them in the oven or in hot oil, (as you prefer)

Bread rolls with ham

We crush the bread well and then we put ham, then we roll it up and only cut into small pieces

Mini hamburger

We need the meat, bread, cheese, if you want to put bacon

We just have to have our meat and bacon cooked and arms our hamburger, put it to heat a little in our pan so that the cheese melts and so that the bread is warm
