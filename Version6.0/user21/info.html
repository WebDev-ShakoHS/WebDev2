Roles

-Owner: Responsible for creating and managing main parts of the discord server that they own (More Info in TOS) -Co-Owner: Responsible for helping out the Owner on creating/managing the discord server, and helps moderators with kicking and banning (More Info in TOS) -Moderator: Responsible for enforcing rules upon discord members and to keep peace within the servers (More Info in TOS) -Server Booster: This role gives you permissions to upload custom emojis to the discord server that you can use anywhere -Temporary Member: This role is automatically given to you once you join, and allows you to few the server's basic functions. Using the roles channel will allow you to gain more access to the discord server -Temporarily Banned: This role is given to members that may have broken the rules without gaining an infraction. Members with these roles will have to wait up to 2 days for removal of this role. This role doesn't let you chat with members and gives you less access that Temporary Members
