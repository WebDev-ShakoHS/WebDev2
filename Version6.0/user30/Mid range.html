Mid Range Cars

The engines that are in mid range cars can range from 5-cylinder engines all the way up to V8 engines. Most of these cars are realitvely easy to modify and tune to user preference. Some fo these cars can reach 0-60 in less than 3 seconds! Tesla is also considered in the mid range performance car talk. Even though they don't have a flashy grill with a V8 inside, they are faster than most of the gas cars at their given price point. The Model 3 Performance Battery can reach 0-60 in just above 3 seconds.

Some of the popular brands for mid range performance cars include Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Dodge, Cadillac, and Tesla
